Project:

For the Capython, my team and I had Challenge as a goal for our project. With that in mind we decided to create a sort of game/website with physical, mental and social challenges that the user has to overcome. The project has cards with the challenges that, once completed, can be turned to reveal reward posters, which we hope would encourage people to complete the challenges. In addition, we created a website to help people choose what challenge category to pick as well to help track different tasks.

HOS.1.1 MANAGE TASKS AND DEADLINES

LEVEL 6:

With support from my teacher, I can keep track of my tasks and my due dates. Less than 10% of my tasks that are overdue or incomplete. I let my teacher know when I need help or when I need more time to turn in work before the deadline.

LEVEL 8:

I can keep track of my tasks and due dates on my own. Almost none of my tasks are overdue or incomplete. I can finish an important task 24 hours before it is due. I integrate constructive feedback from my peers or teachers into some of my most important work before I turn it in.

SELF ASSESSMENT: Level 6-8

I like to think I’m pretty organized when it comes to the things I have to do. I keep track of pretty much everything on google calendar so my tasks are very rarely late. However I do struggle a lot with procrastination and normally do my tasks last minute so it’s what I want to work on the most.
